👋 Hi! I'm Mustafa Uğur Baskın, a 22-year-old Full Stack Developer and Physics undergraduate based in Turkey. I am proud to be among the <b>first Turkish undergraduates</b> to publish two <b>international SCIE-indexed journal</b> in Physics while still pursuing my bachelor's degree. 

### 👨‍🏫 Experience

- **1002-A Project Researcher | The Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey (TÜBİTAK) *(February 2026 - Present)***:
  - Curated an experimental dataset of **2284** binary oxide glass compositions across **16** systems from **SciGlass** records andprepared train/test splits for supervised learning.
  - Built an **XCOM**-based workflow to compute energy-dependent shielding descriptors on a 25-point photon-energy grid (0.015–15 MeV), producing attenuation and thickness-related indicators for screening.
  - Developed a multi-output machine learning model that predicts both a glass-forming confidence score (a **0–100** score indicating how strongly a composition is supported by published glass-formation data) and the shielding targets.
  - Ranked candidate compositions by combining **HVL at 1 MeV** with the glass-forming confidence score to shortlist options that balance glass-forming feasibility and shielding performance.

- **Intern Student | Sakarya University, Faculty of Sciences *(May 2024 - August 2024)***:
  - Developed AI models in Python (PyTorch) to predict the mass attenuation coefficient (MAC) of glass samples to estimate new compositions rapidly.
  - Designed and optimized data pipelines for preprocessing, validation, and comparative analysis, improving modelreliability and evaluation consistency.Supported two SCIE-indexed publications in the Journal of Radiation Research and Applied Sciences.
  - Prepared datasets, developed and tested machine learning models, and improved training workflows for radiation-physics studies.
